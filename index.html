I am the chairman of the student game developer club Acagamics e.V., in Magdeburg. I study computer science at Otto-von-Guericke university and am a software developer at Eudemonia Solutions AG.

I already organized countless events (especially Game Jams) for Acagamics e.V. and volunteered at many conferences like Quo Vadis, A Maze, RESPAWN and c3. I am also the volunteer coordinator for Castle Game Jam 2018.

In my free time I travel around the world and love to photograph.
